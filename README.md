# How Do I Reply To This?

**The AI-powered reply assistant that helps you respond to difficult messages with confidence.**

Live at [replycraft.in](https://replycraft.in)

---

## What It Does

Paste any message you've received — a tough work email, an awkward text, a tricky social media comment — and get 3 ready-to-send replies instantly. Each reply matches a different tone so you can pick the one that feels right.

## How It Works

1. **Paste the message** you need to reply to
2. **Select who it's from** — boss, colleague, client, friend, partner, relative, landlord, or stranger
3. **Pick your desired outcome** — hold a boundary, keep the peace, clarify, decline, assert yourself, or buy time
4. **Get 3 replies** in different tones, ready to copy and send

That's it. One click to copy, and you're done.

## Features

- **3 Tone Options** — Every reply comes in three tones (e.g. warm & friendly, direct & clear, thoughtful & diplomatic) so you always have choices
- **Smart Detection** — Automatically detects who the message is from and suggests the best outcome
- **Reply Length Control** — Toggle between short, medium, and long replies depending on what the situation calls for
- **Message Analysis** — Get a quick breakdown of the sender's tone, intent, and emotional undertone before you reply
- **Smart Suggestions** — Context-aware reply strategies suggested based on the message content
- **One-Click Copy** — Copy any reply to your clipboard instantly
- **Regenerate** — Not happy with a reply? Regenerate individual tones with one click
- **Reply History** — Your recent replies are saved locally so you can revisit them anytime
- **Email Mode** — Switch to email format with subject lines for professional correspondence
- **Example Messages** — Not sure what to try? Pick from built-in examples to see it in action

## Privacy

Your messages are never stored or logged on our servers. Everything is processed in real-time and discarded immediately. Reply history is saved only in your browser's local storage — we never see it.
